# Customer Retention & Churn Analysis Report

## Objective
Analyze customer data to identify churn patterns and retention trends.

## Findings
- Customers with shorter tenure have higher churn rates.
- Customer engagement improves retention.
- Loyal customers generate higher lifetime value.

## Recommendations
- Improve customer onboarding.
- Offer loyalty rewards.
- Provide personalized customer support.

## Conclusion
Retention strategies can reduce churn and increase customer satisfaction and revenue.
